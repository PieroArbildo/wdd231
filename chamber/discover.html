Lima

  • The population of Lima is approximately 9.7 million people, making it the most populous city in Peru and one of the largest in South America.
  • Lima has a total area of around 2,672 km².
  • The population density in Lima is about 3,629 inhabitants per km².
  • The median age of the population in Lima is around 29 years.
  • Lima has a diverse population, with a mix of different ethnic groups, including mestizos (descendants of Europeans and indigenous people), Europeans, Africans, Asians (mainly Chinese and Japanese), and indigenous groups.
  • The official and most spoken language is Spanish.
  • Lima is the economic and financial center of Peru, with a diversified economy that includes sectors such as manufacturing, commerce, financial services, technology, and tourism.
